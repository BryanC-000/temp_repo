About Us

We are a group of final-year computer science students majoring in data science from Monash University Malaysia. We are passionate individuals who enjoy building models to solve real-world problems. For our final-year project, our team built this breast cancer classification model is hopes that it can be used as a diagnostic tool for breast cancer based on breast histology images.
